Sithum Raigamage

I am an enthusiastic B.Eng student majoring in Software Engineering at the Institute of Informatics Technology in partnership with the University of Westminster. Currently in my second year, I have been delving deep into various aspects of software development. In the first semester, I focused on learning database management with MySQL, delving into Object-Oriented Programming with Java, and mastering software engineering principles and practices. In the ongoing second semester, I am immersing myself in the realms of Machine Learning using R, exploring Mobile Application Development with Kotlin, and honing my skills in algorithms and data structures. My journey thus far has equipped me with solid proficiency in Python, Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am eager to continue expanding my knowledge and contributing meaningfully to the world of technology.
